About

4C Construction is a trusted general contractor serving homeowners throughout Omaha, NE and surrounding communities. We specialize in custom home building, whole-home remodeling, basement finishing, kitchen and bathroom renovations, deck construction, and custom carpentry — designed for comfort, quality, and long-term value.

With over 30 years of professional construction experience, our licensed, insured, and bonded team focuses on quality craftsmanship, clear communication, and clean, professional installations. We provide organized job sites and results built to last for homeowners who want the job done the right way.

Our name reflects our core values: Customer, Cost, Communication & Courtesy. From the status of the permit application to the final walkthrough, we work to ensure our customers are completely satisfied every step of the way.
